wonplot - in progress

wonplot is a web based 3D graphing program written in Typescript and THREEjs. In order to plot complex mathematical expressions, wonplot first parses math into ASTs using Dijkstra's Shunting Yard Algorithm before analyzing the equation structure.

Shakespeare Role Assigner

Built for a high school English teacher who struggled to assign students roles which did not conflict with each other (have lines in the same scene) in class readings of Shakspeare, this webapp treats Shakespeare as a graph and attempts to find the best possible graph coloring which equalizes lines and minimizes conflicts per actor.

GUTS!

GUTS! is a probabilistic dice game in which two players take turns rolling two dice until they either choose to end their turn or roll snake eyes. If they choose to end their turn the running total they have earned in that turn is added to their bank. If their turn is ended by snake eyes they loose their running total. First player to 100 points wins. Using value iteration to converge on the optimal strategy, I implemented an AI which, in a round robin tournament between my teacher's AI and the AIs of past and present students, won first place.
